Research Vision & Innovation

As a dedicated PhD candidate at Georgia Institute of Technology, I work at the forefront of supply chain innovation under the mentorship of Dr. Benoit Montreuil. My research transcends traditional boundaries, developing revolutionary protocols and simulation models that reshape how we understand multi-echelon supply chain cooperation.

Currently leading groundbreaking work in Net Zero Freight Systems, I'm pioneering sustainable practices that will define the future of freight transportation. My research encompasses alternative fuel integration, advanced vehicle electrification strategies, and intelligent route optimization algorithms that deliver both environmental and economic value.

My portfolio spans transformative projects with industry leaders including Steelcase Inc., where I revolutionized regional distribution center operations, and pioneering autonomous supply systems during the COVID-19 pandemic. Through partnerships with SF-Express and UPS, I've reimagined multinational logistics networks and multimodal transportation systems, consistently delivering breakthrough solutions that bridge academic excellence with real-world impact.
